The Pipeline

Four steps. You approve every one.

01
Your Work

Your Chapter

Paste your chapter and tell us what the project is about. Add any notes for the reviewers. Your context carries over to every chapter.

02
3 AIs in parallel

Review

Three independent reviewers read your chapter simultaneously. What they check depends on your genre — fiction gets continuity and craft notes, non-fiction gets fact-checking and source verification.

03
AI Synthesis

Final Edit

All three reviews are weighed together. Each suggestion is accepted, declined, or deferred. You get a revised chapter, a decision log, and a change summary. Your notes override everything.

04
Export

Export

Download your finished chapter as a text file, ready for KDP, IngramSpark, or any publisher. Formatted and clean.

Features

Built for writers who care about accuracy.

✍

Human-in-the-loop

Nothing moves to the next step without your explicit approval. Edit, reject, or redo at any point.

📚

Genre-aware prompts

History, theology, fiction, journalism — each genre has tailored prompts for writing, fact-checking, and editorial review.

🌍

Afrikaans support

Write in English, Afrikaans, or both. Dual-language projects export two separate .docx files.

🔒

Fully local

Your projects, API keys, and drafts live on your machine. Nothing is stored on a server. Nothing is shared.

📖

Multi-project

Work on multiple books simultaneously. Each project has its own genre, language, structure, and pipeline state.

⚡

Desktop app

Runs as a native Windows application. No browser tabs, no cloud dependency, no account required.

Genres

Prompts tuned for your discipline.

History

Factual, restrained, sourced. No opinion beyond what the evidence supports. Ends with a "what this period established" summary.

Theology

Intellectual rigour and pastoral warmth. Scripture as primary lens. Distinguishes text from interpretation.

Fiction

Character, voice, tension, pacing. Consistency editing for continuity. Show, don't tell.

Journalism

Lead with what matters. Every claim sourced. No sensationalism. Clear attribution throughout.

Custom

Add your own genre with tailored prompts for every pipeline step. The UI picks it up automatically.
